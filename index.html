About Us

Welcome to University Commerce College

The University Commerce College, established on December 13, 1956, is a prestigious institution with a rich history and academic excellence in Commerce and Management Education.

  • Recognized by the University Grant Commission as "College with Potential for Excellence (CPE)" in 2011.
  • Offers traditional courses like B.Com. Pass Course and B.Com. Honours, as well as professional courses like BBA & BCA.
  • Approximately 5031 students, including those in BBA and BCA courses.
  • Features three computer labs, a smart class room, a language lab, and a library with 1.76 lakhs books.
  • Boasts two large playgrounds and a Bhartiya Chintan Anusandhan Centre with a vast collection of books and videos on Indian thinkers.
  • Highlights Career Counseling and Placement Cell aiding students in employment opportunities.
